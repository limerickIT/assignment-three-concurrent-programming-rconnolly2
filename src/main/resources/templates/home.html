Our Story

Founded in 2025, Zelora is a sustainable online clothing brand based in Ireland, created to celebrate local craftsmanship while promoting an eco-conscious lifestyle. Our collections are crafted with ethically sourced materials, designed for longevity, and made for modern, style-driven consumers across Ireland and Europe.
